Fast Fashion is Costing the Earth

The textile and fashion industry is the second most polluting industry in the world, responsible for 8–10% of total carbon emissions and 20% of global wastewater — with a predicted 50% increase in greenhouse gas emissions by 2030.

Tons of fabrics are wasted, harmful dyes are released into soil and water, and synthetic fibres shed microplastics into our oceans with every wash.

Our aim is to enlighten our audience about their impact on the environment through their fashion footprint — and suggest practical measures toward a sustainable lifestyle.
